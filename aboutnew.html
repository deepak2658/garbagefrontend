GARBAGE FREE INFO PAGE

WE SOLVE THIS

  1. Unavailability of garbage trucks in your area
  2. Overflow of the street dustbins

SOLUTION

The location of all the dustbins in the area will be marked on maps so that in the absence of the garbage collecting vehicles one can easily throw it in the nearby dustbins.For tracking we use GPS which will provide exact location of garbage trucks

IMPLEMENTATION

The garbage level of dustbins are monitored and messages are sent to users.Dustbins which are about to fill are governed by municipality members are intimated to truck drivers for collection.People can also look for alternative dustbins nearby
